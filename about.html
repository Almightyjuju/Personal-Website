Boxing

I first began to box in 2012 wen I was 11 Years old. I weighed no more then 55 lbs. I was told I was too small to compete and it crushed my spirit. Five years later on my 16th birthday I decided to walk back into the gym and prove everyone wrong. I pushed myself and worked twice as hard as the person next to me. I made the amature team, won Louisiana Golden Gloves in 2020, and a year later I turned pro. I am now 3-1 as a professional with 2 kos.

I owe it all to boxing. Boxing has opended many doors and opportunities, like allowing me to compete in 12 different states it has even brought me all the way to Cartagena, Colombia were I competed twice as a pro. It has shaped me into the person I am today. It has taught me to never give up to always chase what's important to you and without hard work, prayer is nothing. It has taught me to be disciplined, patient and honest with myself.

Hiking is also a great way to stay active and challange myself physically. Whether its conquering a steep trail or climbing a tree, hiking always brings a sense of adventure and peace.
